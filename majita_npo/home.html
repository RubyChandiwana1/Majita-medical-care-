Welcome to Fonder Npo

What do we do?

Fonder NPO is a small to medium-sized enterprise, established in 2016, which was initially registered as an NPO. Founded by medical doctors to make a positive contribution to community-level health services provision, Fonder NPO works towards uplifting the health and socioeconomic well-being of the nation. Fonder is proposing a team of highly qualified clinicians currently at the forefront of South African health provision activities.
