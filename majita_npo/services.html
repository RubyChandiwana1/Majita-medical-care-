About Our Service

Fonder NPO offers safe, professional, and accessible medical male circumcision services as part of our commitment to improving community health. Our services are designed to provide high-quality care in a supportive and respectful environment.

Medical Male circumcision

Why Circumcision?

Medical male circumcision is a procedure that has been shown to reduce the risk of certain infections and improve overall hygiene. It is an important part of preventive healthcare, and at Fonder, we ensure that the procedure is performed safely and with the utmost care.

Our Approach

At Fonder, our medical male circumcision services are carried out by experienced healthcare professionals. We follow strict medical guidelines to ensure the safety and well-being of every patient. Our approach is centered on providing compassionate care, with a focus on:

  • Safety: Adhering to the highest medical standards to minimize risks.
  • Comfort: Ensuring that the procedure is as painless and stress-free as possible.
  • Education: Providing patients and their families with comprehensive information about the procedure, benefits, and aftercare.
  • Confidentiality: Respecting the privacy and dignity of every individual.

Who Can Benefit?

Our circumcision services are available to males of all ages, from infants to adults. Whether for medical, cultural, or personal reasons, we are here to provide the care you need in a supportive environment.

How to Access Our Services

If you or your family members are interested in our medical male circumcision services, please contact us to schedule an appointment. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs and ensure that you receive the care you deserve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the procedure painful?
A: We use local anesthesia to minimize discomfort during the procedure. Most patients report only mild discomfort during recovery.

Q: How long does the procedure take?
A: The procedure typically takes about 30 minutes, and patients can go home the same day.

Q: What is the recovery process like?
A: Recovery usually takes about a week. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure a smooth recovery.

If you have any other questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.

HIV & AIDS Services

About Our HIV & AIDS Services

Fonder NPO is committed to providing comprehensive care and support for individuals affected by HIV and AIDS. Our services are designed to promote health, well-being, and dignity, ensuring that everyone has access to the treatment and support they need.

Our Approach

At Fonder, we adopt a holistic approach to HIV and AIDS care, focusing on prevention, treatment, and support. Our services include:

  • Testing and Counseling: Confidential HIV testing and counseling to help individuals know their status and make informed decisions.
  • Treatment: Access to antiretroviral therapy (ART) and ongoing medical care to manage HIV and improve quality of life.
  • Prevention: Education and resources to help reduce the risk of HIV transmission, including PrEP (pre-exposure prophylaxis) and safer sex practices.
  • Support Services: Emotional and psychological support, peer support groups, and community resources to help individuals cope with the challenges of living with HIV and AIDS.

Who Can Access Our Services?

Our HIV and AIDS services are available to anyone who needs them, regardless of age, gender, or background. Whether you are living with HIV, at risk of infection, or seeking support for a loved one, Fonder is here to help.

Why Choose Fonder?

We understand that living with or being at risk of HIV and AIDS can be challenging. At Fonder, we prioritize:

  • Compassionate Care: Our team is dedicated to providing non-judgmental and supportive care to all individuals.
  • Confidentiality: We ensure that all services are provided in a confidential and secure environment.
  • Comprehensive Services: From testing to treatment and support, we offer a full range of services to meet your needs.
  • Community Engagement: We work closely with communities to raise awareness, reduce stigma, and promote healthy practices.

How to Access Our HIV & AIDS Services

If you or someone you know could benefit from our HIV and AIDS services, please reach out to us to schedule an appointment or learn more about our programs. We offer flexible appointment times and a welcoming environment.
